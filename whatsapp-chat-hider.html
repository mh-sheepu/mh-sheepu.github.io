Hide names and sensitive information from WhatsApp chat exports for privacy

How to use:
  • Export your WhatsApp chat (without media) from WhatsApp
  • Paste the text or upload the exported .txt file
  • Select which information you want to hide
  • Click "Process Chat" to anonymize
  • Copy or download the sanitized chat

Note: All processing happens locally in your browser. Your chat data is never sent to any server.
